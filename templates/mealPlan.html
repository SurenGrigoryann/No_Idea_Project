  • 200g salmon fillet
  • 1 large sweet potato

  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 tbsp fresh dill
  • 1 tbsp fresh parsley
  • Lemon juice, salt, pepper
  1. Preheat oven to 200°C (400°F).
  2. Cube sweet potato, toss in 1 tbsp olive oil, roast for 25 min.

Baked Chicken Thighs & Vegetables
Calories
730
Protein
60g
Carbs
65g
Fat
26g
  • 4 chicken thighs (bone-in, skin-on)
  • 1 cup broccoli
  • 1 cup carrots (chopped)
  • 1 zucchini (sliced)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Salt, pepper, fresh thyme
  1. Preheat oven to 220°C (425°F).
  2. Rub chicken thighs with olive oil, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  3. Arrange chicken and vegetables on a baking sheet.
  4. Drizzle vegetables with remaining olive oil and season.
  5. Bake for 35-40 min until chicken skin is crispy and internal temp reaches 75°C.
  6. Rest 5 minutes before serving.
